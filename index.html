My Incredible Works

This is an intro to Javascript project I made on Khan Academy during my early stages of learning. The idea is that when the user clicks on the canvas, a fish with random size and color will be drawn on the position of the cursor. This lesson is about functions and how to call them with parameters. In this case, the parameter is the x and y position, which is provided by the user's mouse click. This worked out pretty well and I even took a screen shot of it and used it as my avatar for many websites. It's a piece of art made with Processing JS.
